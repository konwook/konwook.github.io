I'm a second-year undergraduate student at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in computer science with a concentration in machine learning and minor in mathematics. 

I'm currently an undergraduate researcher at Learning for Embodied Action & Perception Lab under [Professor Deepak Pathak](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~dpathak/). My research interests include topics in vision, few shot learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.

Previously, I was a teaching assistant for [15-251](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~./15251/), CMU's introductory course on theoretical computer science. In my free time, I enjoy playing chess, table tennis, and competitive programming.
